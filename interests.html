Travel

I enjoy traveling to different places and chat with local people to gain more perspectives about the world. I have also travelled to all of the provinces on the East Cost of Canada (Quebec and the Maritimes). I am also no stranger to the United States, I have also travelled to many states in the USA. My most recent trip is to Green Island (綠島) in Taiwan. A graduation trip that I planned for myself to embrace my achievement of successfully finishing my undergraduate career.

Networking

I have been a part of few student-run clubs at UBC as I believe this will help me to broaden my friend circle by finding friends with similar interests or cultural backgrounds. As an executive and and an active member of New Taiwanese Generations, a student-run social club made up by mostly Taiwanese students that grew up in Canada (like myself), I have participated in numerous social events to meet people I can culturally connect with and took on the leadership role to plan countless networking events. I am also very active within the expo/conference community as I believe participating in these events help to me to expand my horizon in fields I relate to and meet people I can strongly connect with.
